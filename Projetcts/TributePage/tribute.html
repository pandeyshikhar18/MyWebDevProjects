Just Fontaine

The man who left his mark on football history

Here's a timeline of Just Fontaine's life:

  • 1933 - Born in Marrakech, Morocco.
  • 1953 - Begins his professional career with the football club USM Casablanca.
  • 1956 - Transfers to OGC Nice in France and quickly becomes one of the league's top scorers.
  • 1958 - Participates in the FIFA World Cup in Sweden, setting a record of 13 goals in a single tournament, a record that still stands today.
  • 1958 - His career is unfortunately cut short due to a serious injury, forcing him into early retirement.
  • 2003 - Receives the Legion of Honor in France for his outstanding contribution to football.
  • 2019 - Honored in the World Football Hall of Fame.

"Fontaine's career is clear evidence that talent, perseverance, and passion can leave an indelible mark on the history of sports."

-- Former French footballer Zinedine Zidane
